About ArbiRich

Automated Cryptocurrency Arbitrage Platform

Project Overview

ArbiRich is a sophisticated cryptocurrency arbitrage platform capable of running multiple trading strategies simultaneously. The system continuously monitors price discrepancies across various exchanges and automatically executes profitable arbitrage opportunities based on predefined criteria.

By leveraging parallel strategy execution, ArbiRich maximizes profit potential while distributing risk across different market conditions and exchange combinations. Each strategy is independently configurable with its own risk parameters and execution logic.

Key Features

Multi-Strategy Engine

Concurrent execution of diverse arbitrage strategies across multiple cryptocurrency pairs and exchanges.

Performance Analytics

Comprehensive metrics and visualizations tracking performance of individual strategies and the overall portfolio.

Risk Management

Sophisticated risk controls including position sizing, execution timing, and automated circuit breakers for each strategy.

High-Performance Infrastructure

Low-latency execution system built with Bytewax data processing pipelines for real-time market monitoring and efficient trade execution across exchanges.
